Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk the eggs and Parmesan together.
  4. Combine everything and serve hot.

Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken (cubed)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, cumin, garam masala, chili powder, lemon juice, and salt. Coat the chicken and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook the Chicken: Grill or pan-fry until cooked and slightly charred. Set aside.
  3. Prepare the Masala: In a pan, heat butter, sauté onions until golden, then add tomato puree, salt, and spices. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Combine & Simmer: Add the cooked chicken, mix well, and pour in heavy cream. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with naan or rice.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a cake pan.
  2. Mix dry ingredients (flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, salt).
  3. Add eggs, oil, milk, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour into the pan and bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. Cool, then enjoy!
